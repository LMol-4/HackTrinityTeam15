About Us

Our Project

As students, finding suitable accommodation has always been a challenge, especially with the ongoing housing crisis in Dublin. Oftentimes, the available options we do find come with poor living conditions. With RentaLyze, we aim to address this overlooked issue, not just for students but for renters nationwide. Our goal is to help improve living conditions and simplify tenant-landlord disputes, making the rental experience fairer and more accessible for everyone.
