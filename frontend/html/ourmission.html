Our Mission

Our mission is to empower tenants in Ireland by providing a simple and effective way to address rental issues through formal complaint letters generated by AI. By allowing tenants to input their rental contract and key details, we help them create well-structured letters that facilitate conflict resolution before legal action becomes necessary. In a challenging rental market where landlords may lack incentive to resolve problems, our goal is to foster fair communication and support tenants' rights, contributing to a more just housing environment.

Our AI Solutions

The Contract PDF is searched and analyzed by our highly specialised AI model, to look for any relevant clauses in relation to the problems provided. It will then generate a letter or a contract based on clauses found or any information the AI deems to be beneficial. It then creates a PDF of the letter or contract to be downloaded and sent to the relevant entities.
