Our Mission

Empowering Irish tenants to effectively address rental issues through AI-generated, clause-based formal complaint letters. By enabling tenants to input their rental contracts and key details, Rentalyze assists in crafting well-structured letters that facilitate conflict resolution before legal action becomes necessary. In Ireland's challenging rental market, where landlords may lack the incentive to resolve problems, our goal is to promote fair communication and uphold tenants' rights, contributing to a more just and equitable housing environment.
